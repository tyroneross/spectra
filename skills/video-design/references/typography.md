# Typography (quick card)

Full prose + research citations: `SOURCE-GUIDE.md` Part III.

## Redundancy rule
On-screen text must NOT verbatim-repeat the VO — the brain can't read and listen to
identical words simultaneously (Redundancy Effect → lower retention). Text should
either carry a message the VO doesn't speak, reinforce a keyword (not a full
sentence), or anchor sound-off viewing.
**Exception:** spatially-integrated callouts (arrows/highlights directly ON the UI
element) reduce cognitive load even when redundant with narration — the signal must
sit on/near the element, not in a separate caption bar.

## Max words per frame
| Context | Max words |
|---|---|
| Marketing/social | 5–15 (6 = optimal thumb-scroll hook) |
| Product demo overlay | 10–20 |
| Tutorial step label | 5–10 (appear before the action, not after) |
| Slide-based explainer | 20 ideal, 40 max |
| Caption/subtitle line | ≤30 chars, ≤3 lines on screen at once |

## Font-size floors (Full HD, 1920×1080)
- **Body/caption:** 40–60px (58px = scientific floor for smartphone-distance acuity).
- **Headline/callout:** 60–90px (≥50% larger than body).
- **Slides:** 32pt+ accessible, 24pt absolute floor.
- **9:16 vertical:** size up 20–30% over the above — smaller screens, arm's length.
- **Font choice:** dynamic sans-serif (Inter, SF Pro Display, Open Sans) — holds up under compression.

## Dwell time
- Minimum 1s per 13 characters (a 30-char line needs ≥2.3s).
- For kinetic/animated text: dwell clock starts AFTER the animate-in finishes — the
  animation itself doesn't count toward reading time.
- If the cut lands before a viewer could read it twice, it's too long or too fast.

## Safe zones (keep text/logos/CTAs out of platform UI)
| Platform | Keep within | Bottom buffer |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok | 900×1492 centered (1080×1920) | ≥320px |
| Instagram Reels | 1010×1280 centered | ≥250px |
| YouTube Shorts | 984px wide, start y:120 | ≥400px |
| LinkedIn (mobile) | center 85%, 15% buffer all sides | varies |
| YouTube 16:9 | center 80% | 80px (subtitles) |
| App Store video | no text allowed at all | N/A |

Universal vertical safe zone: center 900×1400 within 1080×1920.

## Kinetic typography
Sync to beat/VO rhythm; one concept animates at a time; consistent animation style
(vary timing/emphasis, not the effect); entry speed matches energy (slow fade = calm,
fast snap = urgency); hold the key metric 2–3x longer than surrounding words.
